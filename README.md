MEXC lists over 1,700 tokens and offers futures with leverage up to 200x. That combination creates specific emotional conditions: scarcity urgency (new listings before other exchanges), FOMO on altcoin runs, and the kind of overconfidence that 200x leverage produces in new traders.

## What Helps

The 0% spot maker fee removes one anxiety source: the cost of entry and exit. This matters. In clinical terms, reducing micro-stressors lowers background arousal and improves decision quality. You're less likely to hold a bad position just to "make back the fees" if fees were never a factor.

Withdrawal whitelisting is available. Setting it up takes five minutes and eliminates a whole category of regret scenarios — unauthorized withdrawals, hot-wallet mistakes, impulsive sends.

## Building A Safe Practice

Before depositing, write down three things: the maximum loss you'll accept this month, the asset classes you'll trade, and a rule for when you stop for the day.

Not because MEXC requires it. Because your cortisol levels at 2 AM don't.
